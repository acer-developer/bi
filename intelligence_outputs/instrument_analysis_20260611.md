## What to Pitch Per Instrument

### Term Loan
Most common instrument. Every SME with a term loan from a bank needs a rating. Pitch ACER as the faster, more accessible option.

### Bank Guarantee
High volume, typically short-term. Companies need ratings renewed frequently — ideal for ACER annual retainers.

### Non-Govt Debt
Complex instrument, high value. ACER should target these for revenue — larger deal sizes.

### Long Term Loan
Infrastructure/capex driven. Pitch ACER on deep-sector expertise in manufacturing, infra.

### Letter of Credit
Trade finance driven. Often SME and mid-market — ACER's core target segment.

### Fund-Based Working Capital
Working capital — every company needs this. High-frequency renewal. Build portfolio quickly.

### Non-Fund Based
Bank guarantees, LCs — non-fund exposure. Same company may have both fund + non-fund rated.

### Debentures/Bonds
Capital market instrument. Higher complexity, higher fees. Priority for ACER revenue.

### Short Term Loan
Short tenors, frequent renewal. Best for building client stickiness through regular renewals.
