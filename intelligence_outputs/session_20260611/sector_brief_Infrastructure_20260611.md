## ACER OPPORTUNITY ASSESSMENT

**Rating: HIGH**

**Reasons:**
1. Infrastructure is the highest-volume sector in Infomerics data — raw opportunity is massive
2. 16.1% downgrade rate means many borrowers are unhappy with their current agency
3. Infrastructure companies tend to have recurring ratings needs (annual reviews for each facility)
4. Government infrastructure push (roads, power, water) is generating new entrants needing ratings
5. CARE has historically been dominant in infrastructure — their turnaround complaints are
   a documented entry point for ACER

**Risk:**
- Large project finance deals tend to go to top-4 agencies (CRISIL, ICRA) — ACER should
  focus on Tier 2 infrastructure players (sub-₹500 Cr. projects) where Infomerics/CARE compete
