1. **Chemicals/Pharma is a large, fragmented sector** — 889 rated companies across both files.
   Many are mid-sized manufacturers seeking working capital or bond ratings.

2. **CRISIL dominates Chem/Pharma** (324 companies), but has the highest INC exposure.
   ACER pitch: "CRISIL abandoned your peers — we won't."

3. **404 companies have INC status** — highest rate in any sector.
   Chemicals companies often dispute ratings due to cyclical volatility. INC = switching intent.

4. **142 HIGH urgency** — rating renewal overdue.
   Export-oriented Chem/Pharma companies need current ratings for ECGC / trade finance.

5. **Pharma/Biotech: 282 companies** — faster-growing sub-sector.
   API and formulation companies are rating-active due to export compliance requirements.

- Regulatory pressure drives rating renewals (ECGC, trade finance)
